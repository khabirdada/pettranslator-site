Common questions

Is this actually scientific or is it just AI making things up? +

We use multimodal AI — it looks at video, listens to audio, and weighs the context you provide. The system is prompted using published animal behavioral science to identify physical markers (tail carriage, ear position, vocal pitch, posture) before generating a translation. Every report includes a confidence score so you know how clear the signals were.

Is this a replacement for a vet? +

No. PetTranslator.ai is a behavioral analysis tool, not a medical diagnostic. If your pet shows signs of pain, illness, or sudden behavioral change, see a licensed veterinarian. Our PDF export is designed to help that conversation, not replace it.

What pets does it work with? +

Dogs and cats are fully supported. Other companion animals (rabbits, birds, small mammals) are coming after launch.

How is my pet's video used? +

Your video is processed by our AI partners (OpenAI / Anthropic) under their enterprise privacy terms and is not used for model training. We do not sell, share, or publish your uploads. You can delete any analysis from your account at any time.

When does it launch? +

We're aiming for public launch in late summer 2026. Early-access signups get a heads up 2 weeks before public, plus 3 months of Pro free for the first 500 to join.

Do you have an iOS / Android app? +

Not yet. The web app will work on every modern phone and desktop browser at launch. We'll add native apps once we know exactly which features owners use most.
